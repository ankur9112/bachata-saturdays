    ⭐ Class Schedule Structure

    Level L1: Beginner 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM

    Ideal for absolute beginners. We teach timing, footwork, fundamental steps, and intro connection styling.

    Level L2: Improver 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM

    Requires 3+ months experience. We study Sensual Bachata body waves, styling, and musicality combinations.

    Level L3: Intermediate 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

    Requires 6+ months experience. Focuses on advanced connection, performing cues, speed, and styling.

    Level L4: Performance Batch 8 classes (4 weeks)

    Focused on styling mechanics, fast execution speed, and direct performance choreography routines.

    🙋‍♂️ Frequently Asked Questions

    Schedules, guidelines, parking logistics, and support answered.

    Our Bachata progressive course runs for 6 weeks. Each week builds sequentially on top of the last, taking you comfortably from foundational timing to confident social dancing.
    Classes start strictly on time and proceed for 90 minutes of intensive, high-energy, fun learning.
    Please aim to arrive 5 to 10 minutes early. This gives you time to complete check-in on the roster and settle down so we can kick off right on time!
    Don't stress—life happens! It is completely normal to miss a session. Our team and instructors will ensure you safely catch up on the essential steps and styling movements during the next session.
    Yes! Trials and drop-ins are allowed at ₹700 per session. It’s a great way to experience the energy and vibe before committing to the full progressive course.
    Please wear comfortable, breathable clothing that you can move easily in. For footwear, please bring a clean pair of socks or dedicated dance shoes. Outdoor shoes are strictly not allowed on the studio dance floor to keep it clean and safe for spins.
    🏍️ Two-Wheelers: Bike parking is available directly on-site at the venue.
    🚗 Four-Wheelers: Dedicated car parking is not available on-site. If you are driving, you can conveniently and safely park your car at the nearby Nexus Mall.
    No partner is required at all! We rotate partners frequently throughout the class, which is the best way to learn and build connection. However, if you bring a partner and prefer to dance exclusively with them, just let our team know.

    If you have zero experience, you are in the right spot! This course is designed with absolute beginners in mind—we break down everything from the very first timing count.
