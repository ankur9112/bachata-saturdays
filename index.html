    Bachata Saturdays: Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about your dance journey with Sarishtha & Bristi.

    The Basics: Format & Timing

    How many weeks is the course?
    Our Bachata progressive course runs for 6 weeks. Each week builds on the last, taking you from the fundamentals to confident social dancing.
    What is the duration of each class?
    Classes start strictly on time and go on for 90 minutes of intensive, fun learning.
    What time should I arrive?
    Please aim to arrive 5 minutes early. This gives you time to check in and settle down so we can kick off right on time.
    What if I have to miss a class?
    Don't stress—life happens! It is completely plausible to miss one class. Our team and instructors will ensure you catch up on the essential steps during the next session.

    Drop-ins & Trials

    Can I attend a single session or try a class first?
    Yes! Trials and drop-ins are allowed at ₹650 per session. It’s a great way to experience the vibe before committing.

    What to Wear & Bring

    What footwear should I bring?
    Please bring a pair of socks or your dedicated dance shoes. To keep the studio floor clean and safe for spinning, outdoor shoes are not allowed on the dance floor.
    What should I wear?
    Comfortable, breathable clothing that you can move easily in. Standard casual wear or activewear is perfect.

    Parking & Logistics

    Is parking available at the venue?

    🏍️ Two-Wheelers: Bike parking is easily available directly at the venue location.

    🚗 Four-Wheelers: Car parking is not available on-site. If you are driving, you can conveniently park your car at the nearby Nexus Mall parking space.

    Partners & Experience

    Do I need to bring a partner?
    No partner required! We rotate partners frequently throughout the class, which is the best way to learn. However, if you bring a partner and prefer to dance exclusively with them, just let our team know.
    I have zero dance experience. Is this class for me?
    Absolutely. This class is designed with absolute beginners in mind. We break everything down step-by-step from the very first count.
